Our Services

At Mountain Top Mobile RV Repair, we bring 20+ years of expertise directly to your location, providing professional on-site RV repair and maintenance in the Greater Portland Metro area and beyond. Whether you need routine service or urgent repairs, we’ve got you covered.

Our RV Repair & Maintenance Services

Tank Cleaning

Proper tank maintenance is essential for a clean and odor-free RV. We provide black and gray water tank cleaning to prevent buildup, clogs, and foul odors, keeping your system running smoothly.

Appliance Repair & Maintenance

From keeping your fridge cold to ensuring your furnace works in the winter, we service:

  • RV refrigerators
  • Water heaters
  • Furnaces
  • Air conditioners
  • Stovetops & ovens

Electrical & Plumbing Repairs

We troubleshoot and repair electrical and plumbing issues to keep your RV fully functional. Whether you’re dealing with faulty wiring, outlets, water leaks, or pump failures, we’ve got the skills to fix it.

Slide-Out & Leveling System Repairs

A malfunctioning slide-out or leveling system can ruin a trip. We inspect, adjust, and repair hydraulic and electric slide-outs and leveling systems to ensure smooth operation.

Roof & Seal Inspections & Repairs

A leaky roof or damaged seals can lead to costly water damage. We inspect and reseal roofs, windows, and slide-outs to protect your RV from the elements.

General Services

Need routine maintenance or minor repairs? We offer a variety of general RV services to keep your vehicle in top shape, from battery checks and system inspections to minor fixes.
