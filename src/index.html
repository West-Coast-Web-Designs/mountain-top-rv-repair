Expert RV Repair & Maintenance Services

From system diagnostics to appliance repairs, Mountain Top Mobile RV Repair offers comprehensive on-site services to keep your RV in top shape. Wherever you are in the Greater Portland Metro area, we bring 20+ years of expertise straight to you.

  • Appliance Repair & Maintenance

    We service refrigerators, water heaters, furnaces, A/C units, and more.

  • Electrical & Plumbing Repairs

    From wiring issues to leaky pipes, we handle all electrical and plumbing repairs to keep your RV fully functional.

  • Slide-Out & Leveling System Repairs

    Ensure smooth operation of your slide-outs and leveling systems for a hassle-free camping experience.

  • Roof & Seal Inspections & Repairs

    Prevent leaks and water damage with expert roof maintenance, seal inspections, and repairs.

  • Tank Cleaning

    Keep your black and gray water tanks clean and odor-free with professional cleaning and maintenance.

  • General Repair Services

    From routine maintenance to minor fixes, we take care of all your RV service needs—big or small.
